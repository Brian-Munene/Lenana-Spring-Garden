The Building

Beautiful design and character

Keenly selected modern finishes distinguish each element in the apartments, from the main entrance lobby to high-quality residential finishes, crafted flooring and countertops.

Upon entering the main gate, guests and residents find space to breathe before reaching the apartments. Visitor registration beyond the guard house keeps the property secure and well organized.

Community Amenities

Space to work then relax

The courtyard garden on the first floor is designed as a shared extension of home, a place to play with children, meet neighbours or simply relax. The fitness centre is located on the mezzanine floor in a car-free zone, and the residential swimming pool offers an easy place to unwind after long city days.

Apartment Amenities

Bright and beautiful

The apartments include insulated double-pane windows, open and closed kitchen layouts, polished chrome fixtures, private balconies, quartz kitchen countertops and soft-close cabinet doors and drawers.

Bathrooms are designed for a soothing experience after long city days, with sleek finishes, bronze hardware and Jomoo sanitary products.

Indoor Kids Play Area

Friendly space for families

The children's playroom is bright, friendly and ready for playdates, while the fitness facility supports yoga, stretching, cardio, weightlifting and other wellness routines.
