Kilimani Living

Connected and convenient

Nairobi's Kilimani estate has been ranked at position 28 out of 51 coolest neighbourhoods worldwide. A Time Out survey described Kilimani as the place to find the heart and soul of Nairobian life.

Located approximately 5 kilometres west of Nairobi's central business district within Westlands Sub-county, Kilimani sits north of Woodley and east of Kileleshwa, mainly separated by the Kirichwa River.
